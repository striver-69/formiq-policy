Data we collect:
- Camera: used in real-time to analyse exercise movement. 
  No video or images are stored or transmitted off the device.
- Pose landmark data: processed entirely on-device using MediaPipe. 
  Not stored, not transmitted.
- Workout session data: rep counts, ROM metrics, form scores. 
  Stored locally on your device only using MMKV.
- Body measurements (if body scan feature is used): stored locally only.

Data we do NOT collect:
- We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.
- We do not use analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs.
- We do not share any data with third parties.

Children: This app is not directed at children under 13.
