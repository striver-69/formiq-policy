FormIQ PRIVACY POLICY

AI-Powered Form Coaching  ·  Android

Overview
FormIQ is designed with privacy as a default, not an afterthought. All movement analysis happens entirely on your device using MediaPipe. No video, no images, no biometric data ever leaves your phone. This policy explains exactly what the app touches and why.
Data we access on your device
📷
Camera
Used in real-time to analyse exercise movement during a workout session. Camera frames are processed in memory only — no video or images are recorded, stored, or transmitted off the device at any point.
🦴
Pose landmark data
33 body keypoints per frame are computed on-device using MediaPipe Tasks Vision. These coordinates exist only in memory during an active session. They are never stored, never transmitted, and discarded immediately after each frame is processed.
📊
Workout session data
Rep counts, range-of-motion metrics, form scores, and set summaries are stored locally on your device using MMKV (a local key-value store). This data never leaves your phone and is not backed up to any external service.
📐
Body measurements (body scan feature only)
If you use the body scan feature, estimated measurements are stored locally on your device only. They are not transmitted to any server and not shared with any third party.
Data we do not collect
✓
We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
✓
We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, or advertising SDKs of any kind.
✓
We do not share any data — collected or derived — with any third party, ever.
✓
We do not use cloud processing. All AI inference runs locally on your device using MediaPipe.
Children
FormIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.
Android permissions used
🎥
android.permission.CAMERA
Required to stream live frames for pose detection. Used only while a workout session is active on screen. The app does not access the camera in the background.
